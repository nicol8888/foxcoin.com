What is FoxCoin?

How to Buy FoxCoin (FOX)

  1. Step 1: Get a Solana Wallet

    You'll need a wallet that supports the Solana network, such as Phantom or Solflare. Install it as a browser extension.

  2. Step 2: Fund Your Wallet with SOL

    Buy SOL (Solana) on any major centralized exchange (Binance, Coinbase, Bybit, etc.) and send it to your Phantom/Solflare wallet address.

  3. Step 3: Go to a DEX

    Use a Solana decentralized exchange (DEX) like Raydium or Orca to swap SOL for FOX.

    Or use aggregators for more precise coin information:

    • GeckoTerminal
    • DEXTools
    • Birdeye
    • GMGN.AI
    • Photon Solana

  4. Step 4: Swap SOL for FOX

    On the swap page, paste the FOX contract address (GLkewtq8s2Yr24o5LT5mzzEeccKuSsy8H5RCHaE9uRAd) into the field for the token you want to receive. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Ensure you have enough SOL for network fees.
